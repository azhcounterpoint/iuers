Game Rules:

  • Players take turns playing cards face-up to the center pile
  • When a Jack, Queen, King or Ace is played, the next player must play a certain number of cards (1, 2, 3, or 4 respectively)
  • If they play a face card during this sequence, the challenge passes to the next player
  • If no face card is played, the player who played the last face card wins the pile
  • When doubles (two of same rank) or sandwiches (same rank with one card between) appear, players can slap the pile to win it
  • First player to collect all cards wins!
